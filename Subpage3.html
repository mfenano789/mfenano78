Topic 3

Growing up as a typical boy, I hated football. I couldn't understand the logic behind 22 grown persons running after a ball. Also, my brother would receive strokes of the cane for leaving household chores to go play or watch football with his friends. I preferred cartoons or the Philippine movies. They made more sense to me. My brother and I would always fight over the remote control and try to ration our TV times.
